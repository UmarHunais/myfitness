FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Can I cancel my membership anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your membership at any time with no cancellation fees. We believe in flexible memberships that work for your lifestyle.

Is there a free trial available?

Yes! We offer a 7-day free trial for new members. Come in and experience our world-class facilities before committing to a plan.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Absolutely. You can change your membership plan at any time. Changes take effect at the start of the next billing cycle.

Are group classes included in all plans?

Basic plan includes 2 classes per week. Pro and Elite plans include unlimited group classes including yoga, HIIT, spin, and more.
